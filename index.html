Skolapp is a service for searching and comparing international schools that require an interview for admission. It allows you to look for schools by curriculum, area, tuition fees and other aspects. Our initial goal is to provide you with the most accurate information about each school and give you the opportunity to book an interview for your kid from the comfort of your home. Unlike many other crowd funding sites, we are not looking for your money, but for your word. If you see this service as potenial for helping parents, feel free to share it.

We are located in Egypt and currently in the launch phase. Our website is being actively developed and we are planning to go live on April 2014. If you are interested, sign up and receive our quarterly news letter. We will not spam you and we will never share your email without your consent. Our service is a great tool to save your time finding the right school for your kids.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?
The number of international schhols in Egypt is fairly limited, and we have a couple of talented surveyors who are going to survey and inspect schools in person. We also will make use of our acquaintences to provide you with initial information about each school. When the service attracts more users, we are going to introduce school reviews where parents and students alike can review schools and rate them. Our profit comes from schools, not from parents.
What makes you different from other services?
We are not aware of any site offering the same service for parents, particularly booking an interview. If you know any, please let us know in the contact page.
